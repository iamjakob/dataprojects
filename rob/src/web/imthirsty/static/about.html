ImThirsty: The basics.

The world of microbrewing and craft beer can be just as overwhelming as it is delicious. You know what beers you like, but the sheer volume of choices can leave you stuck in a rut not knowing what to try next. While there are great websites like BeerAdvocate.com giving you loads of information and individual opinions on every beer imaginable, this just makes the problem worse. You are currently drinking a beer. You like it. ImThirsty tells you which you'll like next.
